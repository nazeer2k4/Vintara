Privacy Policy

At Vintara International, we are committed to protecting your privacy and safeguarding your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and secure your data when you interact with our website, products, or services. By using our website, you agree to the practices described below.

1. Company Information

Legal Name: Vintara International
Brand Name: Vintara

2. Information We Collect

We collect information to enhance your experience and deliver improved services. This may include:

  • Personal Identification Information: Name, email address, phone number, billing/shipping address.
  • Transaction Data: Payment details, order history, invoices, and delivery information.
  • Technical Data: IP address, browser type, operating system, and cookies.
  • Marketing Preferences: Your choices regarding newsletters, offers, and updates.

3. How We Use Your Information

Your information helps us to:

  • Process and deliver your orders.
  • Communicate with you regarding inquiries, orders, or support.
  • Provide personalised experiences on our website.
  • Send updates, special offers, and promotional material.
  • Comply with legal and regulatory requirements.

We do not sell, rent, or misuse your personal information. Data is only shared with trusted partners (such as shipping and payment providers) when necessary.

4. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

We use cookies to make your browsing experience smoother. Cookies allow us to:

  • Analyze: Track website traffic and improve performance.
  • Enhance: Remember your preferences and provide tailored content.
  • Advertise: Deliver relevant promotions based on your browsing behavior.

You may disable cookies through your browser settings, but doing so may affect certain website functions.

5. Third-Party Service Providers

To deliver our services efficiently, we may share data with:

  • Payment processors.
  • Logistics and shipping partners.
  • Website analytics and marketing tools.

All third parties we work with are required to protect your data and are restricted from using it for unrelated purposes.

6. Data Security

We implement industry-standard measures such as encryption and secure servers to protect your personal data. While we strive for complete security, no online transmission is 100% risk-free.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access – Request a copy of your personal data.
  • Correct – Update any incorrect or incomplete information.
  • Delete – Request removal of your personal data (where legally possible).
  • Opt-Out – Unsubscribe from marketing communications anytime.

To exercise these rights, please reach out to us directly.

8. Retention of Data

We retain your personal data only for as long as required to fulfill the purpose for which it was collected or as required by law. Once no longer needed, your information will be securely deleted or anonymized.

9. International Data Transfers

If you access our website from outside India, your data may be processed in India or in other countries where our service partners operate. By using our website, you consent to these transfers.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may revise this Privacy Policy occasionally. Updates will be posted here, and continued use of our website will indicate your acceptance of these changes.
